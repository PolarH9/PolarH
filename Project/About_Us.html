Mark Thompson, born and raised in the vibrant tech hub of Silicon Valley, exhibited an early fascination with computers and technology. This interest, fostered by tinkering with his father's old computer parts, evolved into a lifelong passion for understanding the intricacies of hardware and software systems.

As a software engineer at Google, Mark thrived in an environment that encouraged creativity and problem-solving. His contributions to various projects earned him accolades and recognition among his peers, solidifying his reputation as a talented and resourceful engineer.

Over the course of eight years at Google, Mark played a pivotal role in the development of cutting-edge technologies that shaped the digital landscape. From refining search algorithms to enhancing user experience on popular platforms, his work left an indelible mark on the products and services used by millions worldwide.

Despite his success at Google, Mark felt a stirring desire to pursue his entrepreneurial ambitions. His fascination with building custom PCs, which had been a hobby since his university days, beckoned him to explore a new frontier. Thus, armed with a wealth of experience and a burning entrepreneurial spirit, Mark took a leap of faith and founded TITAN PC Builds.
